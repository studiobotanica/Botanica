# 🌿 BOTANICA

> **Your Plants, Identified Instantly.**

BOTANICA is an AI-powered plant identification web app that lets you scan any plant or flower and instantly get its name, care guide, pest diagnosis, and more — powered by Claude AI.

## 🚀 How It Works

1. **Upload or Scan** — Take a photo or upload an existing image of any plant, flower, leaf, or tree.
2. **AI Analysis** — Claude AI analyzes the image against a database of 50,000+ species.
3. **Get Results** — Receive the plant's name, description, care guide, and pest detection.
4. **Mint as NFT** *(PRO)* — Connect your wallet and mint your discovery as a digital collectible.

## 🤝 Why We Built It

Most people encounter plants every day but have no idea what they're looking at. We built BOTANICA to close that gap — giving everyone from curious beginners to passionate gardeners instant, accurate botanical knowledge right from their phone. With Claude AI's vision capabilities and a database of 50,000+ species, we wanted to make plant discovery effortless, educational, and even collectible through Web3 NFT integration.
